ABOUT
US

At Human Interest, our mission is simple: help real people find each other through the things they genuinely care about.

We believe the best connections do not start with a swipe. They start with a shared interest, a common rhythm, a place, a passion, a night out, a class, a song, a table, a conversation. Human Interest was built around that belief.

HI is a verified, next generation social discovery app where people join interest-based communities around what they love — fitness, food, nightlife, music, culture, travel, wellness, and more — then meet, connect, date, and make friends in real life.

We've created a new kind of social platform. One that sits between social networking and dating, but works differently and better than both. Less performative. Less random. Less transactional. More Human.

Every member is verified. Every community is built around a shared interest. Every connection starts with something more meaningful than a photo and a first impression.

Human Interest is the place where real people, shared interests, and real-world connections finally come together.

Your home feed has a daily rhythm to it built to pull you toward people with shared interests and connect with them, not keep you scrolling past them.

Every part of the app is designed for one thing: connection. No ads, no selling your data, no algorithm working against you.

Human Interest is member-supported, so we answer to you — not to advertisers. Other apps are built to keep you looking. We're built to help you find.

That means you do not just browse people. You enter communities. You find your scene. You discover people through shared energy, shared places, shared passions, and shared intent.

Whether you are looking for new friends, a date, a group to go out with, a workout partner, a dinner crew, or simply people who love what you love, HI gives you a better way to find them.

We are not trying to make people spend more time online. We are trying to make the time they spend online lead to something real.

We're starting in New York, one community at a time, because real connection happens locally before it happens anywhere else. Human Interest is where you meet the people who end up mattering.
