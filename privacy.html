1. Overview

By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated through your use of the Platform, and information from third parties.

a. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, username, email address, phone number, profile photo, biography, interests, and preferences.
  • Payment Information: Billing details and transaction information (processed securely by third-party payment processors; we do not store full payment card numbers).
  • Communications: Messages, room participation, feedback, support requests, and other communications you send through the Platform.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Interactions with rooms, channels, features, and content; timestamps; referral sources.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device type, operating system, app version, IP address, browser type, and identifiers.
  • Approximate Location: Derived from IP address (e.g., city or region level), not precise GPS location unless explicitly permitted.

c. Information from Third Parties

  • Affiliates & Partners: Referral or attribution data from affiliates, creators, or community partners.
  • Service Providers: Analytics, hosting, and infrastructure partners that support Platform functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Platform;
  • Create and manage your account and profile;
  • Enable participation in rooms, channels, and community features;
  • Process subscriptions, payments, and revenue sharing;
  • Personalize your experience, including recommending rooms or connections;
  • Support moderation, safety, and community integrity;
  • Communicate with you about updates, support, and important notices;
  • Analyze usage trends and improve performance;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
AI & Automated Systems. Human Interest may use AI-assisted systems to support features such as recommendations, moderation assistance, matching, and product improvement. These systems are designed to enhance user experience and safety and are not used to sell personal data or make solely automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, payments), under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Creators & Affiliates: Limited data necessary to support attribution, revenue sharing, or channel operations.
  • Legal & Safety Reasons: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of Human Interest, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate safeguards.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies to:

  • Authenticate users;
  • Remember preferences;
  • Analyze performance and usage;
  • Measure acquisition and referral effectiveness.

You may manage cookie preferences through your device or browser settings. Some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

You may request account deletion at any time. Upon deletion, we will remove or anonymize your personal data, subject to legal, security, or operational requirements.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable laws (including GDPR and CPRA/CCPA), such as the right to:

  • Access your personal information;
  • Correct inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion;
  • Object to or restrict processing;
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact support@humaninterestapp.com.

9. International Data Transfers

Human Interest is based in the United States. Your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. or other jurisdictions. We use appropriate safeguards to protect your information in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children’s Privacy

Human Interest is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the date above or through the Platform. Continued use of the Platform after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.
