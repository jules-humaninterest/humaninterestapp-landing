Child Safety Standards Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 14, 2026

Our Commitment

Human Interest has a zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that exploits, harms, or endangers children. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and to complying fully with all applicable laws, including those related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).

Zero tolerance: Any user found sharing, distributing, or soliciting CSAE or CSAM content will be immediately banned and reported to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).

Prohibited Content

The following content and behaviors are strictly prohibited on Human Interest:

  • Any sexual content involving minors (individuals under 18 years of age)
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors in any form
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAM or CSAE material
  • Any communication intended to establish inappropriate contact with a minor
  • Content that sexualizes or objectifies minors in any context

Age Requirements

Human Interest is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. We require users to confirm their age upon registration. Accounts found to belong to minors will be removed immediately.

Reporting Mechanism

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates this policy or that you believe puts a child at risk, please report it immediately through one of the following channels:

  • In-app report button available on every profile and conversation
  • Email: safety@humaninterestapp.com
  • NCMEC CyberTipline: missingkids.org/cybertipline

All reports are reviewed promptly. We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations involving child safety.

Enforcement

Upon receiving a report or detecting a violation through our moderation systems, we will:

  • Immediately suspend the reported account pending investigation
  • Preserve relevant data and evidence
  • Report confirmed CSAM to NCMEC and applicable authorities
  • Permanently ban accounts confirmed to have violated this policy

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact our safety team at safety@humaninterestapp.com.
